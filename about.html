Hi, I'm Ed









Based in Providence, Rhode Island, I am pursuing a Master’s in Industrial Design at the Rhode Island School of Design. I previously studied Creative Media at the City University of Hong Kong, and my practice reflects this hybrid background, sitting between art and design.


I’m drawn to political and social questions, to how systems shape people, and how design might challenge those systems rather than simply reinforce them. I don’t work within a fixed medium; my practice spans product, graphic, systems, and concept design, often leaning toward the critical or speculative.
